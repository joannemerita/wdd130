Purpose

The Young Women organization of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints provides instruction, encouragement and support in living the gospel of Jesus Christ for teenage female Church members ages 12 through 17.

Audience

This page was created for informative purposes, so the page is dedicated to the Young Women from the Faucett ward, El Olivar stake. Nevertheless, everyone is free to see what we do as daughters of our Heavenly Father.
